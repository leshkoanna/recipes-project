Sushi

Description

Homemade sushi will never be at the level of sushi from the top sushi restaurants in NYC, but here’s where homemade sushi wins: You get to gorge yourself on a sushi FEAST for like $20, whereas a similar amount of sushi at a restaurant would cost a couple hundred. Soooooo worth it, if you ask me.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • fish
  • seaweed
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Spread the seaweed with a layer of prepared sushi rice, and flatten gently with a rice paddle (but do not smash the rice, as my mom would always say):
  2. Place your desired ingredients onto the rice. I’ve got salmon, avocado, and cream cheese.
  3. Roll it up tightly, using a bamboo mat.
